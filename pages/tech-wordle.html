Tech Wordle 🧩

Guess the 5-letter Tech, Math, or Physics word in 6 tries!

How to Play?

Guess the 5-letter hidden word in 6 tries. Type any word and press Enter. The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was:

A
Green: The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
R
Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
X
Gray: The letter is not in the word at all.
